About 1AI

1AI is the vision of AI for every single person — accessible, intelligent, and built to serve everyone equally. Powered by the Re-Search engine, 1AI uses large language model (LLM) technology to understand your questions and deliver meaningful, conversational answers.

Built as part of the Blue-Star project and the G.O.S.© (Global Operating System), 1AI represents the next generation of AI-driven search that goes beyond simple keyword matching to truly understand and respond to human queries.
